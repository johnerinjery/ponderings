Ponderings

Hello, Reader. My name is John Erinjery. You can find more about me elsewhere, in this space I try to summarize a lot of my Ponderings.In Ponderings I will share the fruits of my thinking sessions on many things, some prominent, others rather obscure.

I made this space for myself simply due to the fact that I am motivated to organize my thoughts and summize them as I am trying to write them up. More often than not, I tend to forget the various trains of thought I had a few weeks in the past, this website was my rather efficiency driven solution for the same.

I have tried to categorize topics the best I can; Some of these are truly trivial - please amuse me.
